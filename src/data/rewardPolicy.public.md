# Rewards & Membership Programme

Our rewards programme is designed to thank loyal customers while preserving the quality and value of our collections.

## Earning Points

Earn points on every full-price purchase (amounts in AZN / ₼, same currency as prices on this site):

- Up to 72 ₼ → 2% back
- 72–180 ₼ → 3.5% back
- 180+ ₼ → 5% back

Points are calculated on regular-price items only.

## Using Your Points

- Points can be used as a discount at checkout
- Points apply only to full-price items
- You can use points partially or fully on eligible items

## Membership Benefits

Your membership level is based on your total spend:

**Silver**
- 3% off full-price items
- Early access to new collections

**Gold**
- 5% off full-price items
- Early access to collections and promotions

**Platinum**
- 8% off full-price items
- Priority service
- Exclusive offers

**Platinum+**
- 10% off full-price items
- Priority service
- Exclusive and personalised offers

## Important Notes

- Points and discounts apply only to full-price items
- Promotions and discounts cannot be combined
- Points are valid for 12 months from the date earned

## Programme Updates

The company reserves the right to modify or terminate the rewards programme at any time without prior notice.
